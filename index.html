Accredited Practising Dietitian

At the heart of my practice is a commitment to helping individuals live their healthiest, happiest lives through personalized, sustainable nutrition solutions. As an Accredited Practising Dietitian, I believe in tailoring my guidance to meet each person’s unique needs, preferences, and health goals. Drawing from my rich experience working with Singapore's Health Promotion Board, I have led numerous workshops, seminars, and cooking demonstrations, empowering my clients with the knowledge to make informed dietary choices. I take a holistic approach to nutrition, integrating not only the science behind food and health but also each individual’s cultural background and personal preferences to create realistic, effective plans. My background, including my time as a flight stewardess with Singapore Airlines, has refined my ability to connect deeply with people from all walks of life. A fun fact, I have a huge passion for travel, exploring new cultures, and I love health and wellness activities; fitness—yoga, top rope climbing, and Pilates-adding to my nutrition counseling. Whether you’re looking to improve your overall well-being, achieve specific health goals, or simply adopt a more balanced lifestyle, I'm here to provide sound, expert advice that fits seamlessly into your life.
