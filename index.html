Accredited Practising Dietitian

I’m an Accredited Practising Dietitian committed to helping you achieve a healthier, happier life through personalized, sustainable nutrition. My approach blends science, lifestyle, and cultural preferences to create realistic meal plans that fit your needs.

With experience at Singapore’s Health Promotion Board, I’ve led workshops and demos to empower informed eating habits. My background as a former flight stewardess allows me to connect with diverse individuals, offering practical, approachable guidance. Passionate about health, fitness, and global cuisines, I believe in balanced nutrition and movement—whether through yoga, climbing, or Pilates.
